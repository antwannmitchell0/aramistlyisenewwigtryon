Got Questions?

We've got
answers.

Everything you need to know about our collection, virtual try-on, and classes.

How does the virtual try-on work?
+
Our virtual try-on uses AI-powered facial landmark detection to map any wig from the Aramis Tylise collection onto your face in real time. Upload a front-facing photo or enable your camera, select a style, and the AI renders it on you — matching your hairline, face shape, and skin tone accurately.
Is virtual try-on really free?
+
Yes — completely free. No account, no subscription, no credit card. Just open the try-on, choose your style, and see yourself in it. We believe you should be confident in your choice before you spend a dollar.
Will it look accurate on my skin tone?
+
Absolutely. Our AI was built with inclusivity from day one. It works across all complexions — deep melanin tones, medium, fair, and everything in between. The rendering adapts to your coloring for an accurate preview, not a one-size overlay.
Is my photo stored or shared?
+
Never. All processing happens on your device — your photo is never uploaded to our servers, stored, or shared with anyone. When you close the try-on session, everything clears. We're fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.
What's the difference between Ready to Ship and Custom Wigs?
+
Ready to Ship units are pre-made premium wigs available for immediate purchase and same-day shipment — they're styled, finished, and ready to wear. Custom Wigs are handcrafted to your personal specifications: cap size, hair type, length, color, and density. Custom orders have a production timeline — contact us for details.
What is Pure Cambodian Hair?
+
Our Pure Cambodian Hair is ethically sourced and imported weft extensions known for their exceptional quality, natural luster, and durability. Cambodian hair is prized for its thickness, low porosity, and ability to blend seamlessly across a wide range of textures. It can be colored, heat-styled, and maintained like your own hair.
What is your return policy?
+
We offer 30-day returns on unworn, unaltered items in original packaging. Custom orders and final sale items are non-returnable. If there's an issue with your order, contact us and we'll make it right. We stand behind every product we sell.
How do I enroll in the Signature Cutting & Styling course?
+
Simply visit the Online Classes page and purchase the course. You'll receive instant access to all materials. The course is fully self-paced with lifetime access, so you can learn on your schedule. For hands-on training, visit the Classes page to view upcoming in-person dates and secure your seat.
How do I find my correct wig size?
+
Visit our Wig Size Guide page for step-by-step measuring instructions. Most of our units feature adjustable straps that accommodate a range of head sizes, but getting your measurements ensures the best fit and most natural-looking wear.
